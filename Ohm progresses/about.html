About Ohm

Welcome to Ohm, where we are committed to revolutionizing the energy industry through sustainable solutions. Our mission is to provide innovative tools and resources to empower individuals and businesses to transition towards a greener and more efficient future.

Ohm's goals

At Ohm, we believe that every action counts towards building a sustainable world. That's why we offer a range of subscription plans tailored to meet the diverse needs of our customers. From basic energy optimization tools to advanced customization options and full-suite business solutions, we have everything you need to embark on your journey towards a cleaner, greener tomorrow.

Join us in our mission to transform the energy landscape and make a positive impact on the planet. Together, we can create a brighter future for generations to come.

History

Ohm was founded in 2015 by a group of passionate engineers with a vision to revolutionize the energy industry. The company started with a mission to provide sustainable and affordable energy solutions to households and businesses worldwide.

In its early days, Ohm focused on research and development, striving to create innovative technologies that would optimize energy consumption and reduce carbon footprint. Through relentless dedication and ingenuity, the team at Ohm developed groundbreaking solutions that would later become the cornerstone of its success.

By 2017, Ohm launched its first product line, introducing smart energy meters and monitoring systems designed to empower consumers with real-time insights into their energy usage. These devices allowed users to track their electricity consumption, identify inefficiencies, and make informed decisions to save energy and lower utility bills.

As Ohm continued to grow, it expanded its product portfolio to include energy-efficient appliances, solar panels, and home battery storage solutions. The company also diversified its services, offering energy consulting, installation, and maintenance to support customers in their journey towards energy independence.

In 2020, Ohm made significant strides in renewable energy innovation by partnering with leading solar panel manufacturers and investing in advanced battery technology. These partnerships enabled Ohm to offer comprehensive solar energy solutions, including rooftop solar installations and grid-tied battery storage systems, further empowering customers to harness clean, sustainable energy from the sun.

Today, Ohm stands as a leader in the renewable energy sector, with a global presence and a reputation for excellence in sustainability and innovation. With a commitment to driving positive change and shaping the future of energy, Ohm continues to push the boundaries of what's possible, delivering cutting-edge solutions that make clean energy accessible to all.
